Flight Experience Moreno

Taking off from El Calafate Airport, the flight offers a unique panorama of the majesty of Patagonia.

Overflying the southern coast of Lake Argentino, passengers witness the impressive size of this body of water, with prominent views of Bahía Redonda and the town of El Calafate.

As the plane progresses, you'll spot the Centinela River and the area of Estancia Anita, passing by Estancia Chorrillo Malo. On clear days, you can enjoy a panoramic view of the Torres del Paine in Chile, an extraordinary sight that takes your breath away.

Continuing the journey, you'll traverse unique landscapes like the Rico River and Lake Roca, before marveling at the impressive distant view of the Perito Moreno Glacier. This spectacular sight is enjoyed without entering Los Glaciares National Park, ensuring the preservation of its natural beauty.

The flight also offers stunning views of Cerro Buenos Aires before exploring the port of Bandera and the mystical Boca del Diablo. At this point in the flight, if the day permits, you may also be able to observe the southern face of El Chalten from a distance of approximately 80 km.

The presence of the Patagonian ranches, surrounded by pleasant landscapes, highlights the importance of the commercial and tourist development of the area.

Concluding the experience, you'll fly over the beautiful Lagunas de Estancia La Unión before returning to the town of El Calafate, completing an hour of flight full of exciting landscapes and unforgettable moments.

The flight may undergo route modifications as deemed necessary by the crew, due to meteorological conditions or any situation that may endanger the safety of passengers or property.

  • Twin-engine aircraft
