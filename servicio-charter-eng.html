Charter flights

A charter flight is an aviation service in which the entire aircraft is rented by a specific group of passengers for a particular journey. Unlike scheduled regular flights, in a charter flight, the aircraft is exclusively reserved for a specific group of people, whether for leisure, business, sports, among others.

Charter flights can be used for a variety of purposes, from group vacation trips to corporate transportation or even medical evacuations. This type of service offers flexibility in terms of schedules and destinations, which can be beneficial for groups wishing to travel together and have specific needs regarding schedules and destinations.

Glacier Sky is a company that offers charter flights to various destinations, including El Chaltén, Puerto Deseado, and Patagonian ranches with declared airstrips, as well as destinations such as Río Gallegos, Comodoro Rivadavia, Puerto Santa Cruz, Perito Moreno, Río Turbio, Río Grande, and Ushuaia. They also offer the possibility of requesting quotes for international charter flights to destinations such as Puerto Natales and Punta Arenas.

Those interested can contact Glacier Sky to request a quote for specific charter flight circuits or itineraries. Glacier Sky strives to provide personalized and safe service, adapting to the individual needs of each client and guaranteeing an exceptional travel experience.
