Strobel Lake

Lake Strobel, located in the heart of the Santa Cruz province, is a highly preferred fishing destination for anglers worldwide. Its reputation is primarily due to the population of rainbow trout, which are renowned for their size and strength.

This internationally renowned destination has its own airstrip, providing quick access in less than an hour and a half flight from El Calafate. Arriving at the destination by plane saves time for the passenger, allowing them to arrive less fatigued and start their fishing activity immediately. This advantage provides them with more available hours for fishing during the day.

Additionally, the natural environment surrounding Lake Strobel is breathtaking, offering visitors the opportunity to enjoy pristine landscapes and unparalleled tranquility. The abundance of wildlife in the area, including waterfowl and terrestrial mammals, adds an extra allure to the fishing experience.

Accommodation and guide services are available for anglers wishing to explore Lake Strobel and its surroundings. Local operators offer a variety of options, from cozy cabins to fully equipped fishing camps, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay for all visitors.

With its unique combination of excellent fishing, convenient access, and stunning natural beauty, Lake Strobel remains one of the most coveted destinations for fishing enthusiasts worldwide.
